YOUR CHALLENGES CAN BE CONFUSING.

Stop temptations, build confidence and overcome challenges

In my 30 years as a therapist, I have found that many challenges are rooted
in painful childhood experiences.

Traditional talk therapy can be prolonged, painful and costly. In contrast,
hypnotherapy is a state of deep relaxation that can bring closure to painful
past experiences and teach you the skills you need to overcome your
challenges. And most of the time, it takes just a few sessions.

You don't need to feel stuck.

Experience inner peace. Make lasting changes.

REAL PEOPLE. REAL TRANSFORMATION

*  

* Kayla

PRESENTING PROBLEM

Kayla was suffering from OCD. Despite
being on 4 different medications, including
an antidepressant, she was still struggling
daily.

THE PROCESS

Through hypnotherapy, Kayla used the
subconscious to deal with unhealthy,
obsessive thoughts and help her mind
accept healthy messages instead.

NUMBER OF SESSIONS

4, plus monthly follow ups.

RESULTS

In a matter of months, Kayla was down to
only 1 medication and looking towards a
hopeful, functional future.

* Michael

PRESENTING PROBLEM

Michael and Ellen were struggling with their
relationship and on the verge of divorce.

THE PROCESS

Through hypnotherapy, Michael recalled an
event he had long forgotten. At the age of 9,
a traumatic incident had taught his
subconscious never to trust others and to
associate close relationships with danger.
This was impacting his ability to forge a
healthy relationship with Ellen.

NUMBER OF SESSIONS

12, plus monthly follow ups.

RESULTS

With awareness of what was holding him
back, Michael was finally able to process
his childhood trauma and begin to build a
healthy, safe marriage.

* Rena

PRESENTING PROBLEM

Rena, a succesful young woman, was
suffering from sudden anxiety attacks.

THE PROCESS

Through hypnotherapy, Rena recalled her

worthlessness and loneliness. We revisited
her inner child and told her she was loved,
smart and supported.

NUMBER OF SESSIONS

5, plus exercises to do at home and monthly
follow ups.

RESULTS

Rena's anxiety attacks disappeared as did
her feelings of worthlessness. Instead she
finally felt free.
