ABOUT MOISHE HERSKOWITZ

Moishe Herskowitz, MS, LCSW, CCH has over 30 years experience as a
therapist. He is also an Adjunct Lecturer with Touro College, VP of the
Association of Orthodox Jewish Teachers of New York City Public Schools,
a Certified Clinical Hypnotherapist and a trainer for Cable Therapy.
Through his work with couples in crisis, and clients suffering from anxiety,
Moishe found that many of the issues clients were dealing with, stemmed
from painful childhood traumas. He began to use hypnotherapy to help
clients identify and heal the pain that was holding them back from living life
optimally, with great success.

As the director of How We Communicate PLLC, Mr. Herskowitz designed a
series of programs aimed at a variety of issues. Using a combination of
traditional therapy and hypnotherapy, Moishe helps clients learn to live
optimally in the present and look hopefully to the future.
